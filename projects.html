HND Final Project

Uros: A Trip Through Shadows

We (Oniric Kittens) are currently working in a game as our final project with Unreal Engine. I'm the programmer in charge of small objects such as fight zones, pickups or interactables. I also programmed the saving and loading game data system and prepared the GameMode and GameInstance. One more thing I'm helping with is the level design of the 4th zone in our game.

Graphic Engine

Titan Engine

I'm developing a graphic engine with my classmate Diego Cosín. Some of the features it has are:

  • Lua scripting.
  • ImGui.
  • Deferred Shading.
  • More than 2000 lights (Directional, Point, Spot).
  • Shadow mapping.
  • Postprocesing.
