HND Final Project

Uros: A Trip Through Shadows

We (Oniric Kittens) are currently working in a game as our final project with Unreal Engine. I'm part of the programmers team and some of the things I programmed for our game are:

  • - Small objects. Pickups and interactables.
  • - Saving and loading game data system.
  • - AI. I worked on 4 of our enemies within the game: the wandering souls (small flying enemies) and one of the mermaids (bigger flying enemies).
  • - Other stuff. I programmed the fight zones and the GameMode and GameInstance base code.

I didn't only program but also designed the whole 4th zone in our game.

Graphic Engine

Titan Engine

I'm developing a graphic engine with my classmate Diego Cosín. Some of the features it has are:

  • - Lua scripting.
  • - ImGui.
  • - Deferred Shading.
  • - More than 2000 lights (Directional, Point).
  • - Shadow mapping.
  • - Postprocesing: HDR Bloom.
  • - PBR.

In the future, we're planning to add:

  • - Spot lights.
  • - Cascaded shadow mapping.
  • - Antialiasing.
